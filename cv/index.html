Research Experience

  • since 03/2023
    Postdoctoral Research Fellow
    Columbia AI and Robotics Lab, Columbia University, New York
    • PI of the FWF project Making Sense of Objects from Exploratory Robotic Interaction, investigating continuous object and interaction learning.
  • 04/2022 - 02/2023
    Postdoctoral Researcher
    Vision for Robotics Lab, TU Wien, Vienna
    • Investigated robot vision methods for transparent & deformable objects in the H2020 project TraceBOT, enabling traceable robotic manipulation.
  • 10/2018 - 03/2022
    Research Assistant
    Vision for Robotics Lab, TU Wien, Vienna
    • Developed object pose estimation and verification methods for robotic grasping using learning-, simulation- and rendering-based approaches in the CHIST-ERA project Heap and the doctoral college TrustRobots.
  • 07/2016 - 08/2016
    Research Intern
    Virtual Reality Group, TU Wien, Vienna - Illusion Walk, Berlin
    • Implemented and optimized a GPU-based multi-marker tracking pipeline for large-scale VR applications, from camera input to pose output.
